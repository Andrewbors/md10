MD10 - preserved heritage creates memory

We are a local company operating in Western Ukraine. We remember the magic that Diego Maradona created on the football field, and of course we were impressed. So we decided to create clothes that will remind us of Diego's greatness from every stage of his career.

This collection consists of 7 styles, symbolizing 7 periods of Maradona's career - 6 clubs and the national team of Argentina. Each style refers to the colors of the T-shirt in which Maradona created the story of each period, and has its own unique slogan that describes it.
